WORK

Am I Rent Stabilized?

- A web app to encourage NYC tenants to find out if their landlord is illegally over charging them for a rent-regulated apartment. - using open data & tech to motivate average people to become activists - there's a monetary incentive for people to check their apartment's status but I decided not to go that route

Language Translations

- multi-lingual was important - translated all of the site's content to spanish and chinese (next two most commonly spoken languages in NYC)

Goals:

  • Educate NYC tenants about rent regulation
  • Motivate them to take action
